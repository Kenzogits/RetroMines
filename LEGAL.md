1. OWNERSHIP & INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY 
The RetroMines application, including its specialized Minesweeper game logic, "Crypto Snake" legacy modules, user interface designs, and exclusive retro-style graphical assets, is the sole and exclusive property of the RetroMines Development Team. This declaration serves as the official confirmation of ownership and proprietary rights for the application's distribution, specifically covering the package com.crypto.retromines.


2. TRANSPARENCY & DECENTRALIZED LOGIC 
We are committed to the highest standards of transparency. The application's core logic, including the integration of the Solana Mobile Wallet Adapter, is designed to interact directly with the Solana blockchain. This architecture ensures that all on-chain interactions and record-binding for leaderboards are verifiable and secure for the user.


3. NON-CUSTODIAL COMPLIANCE
Non-Custodial Nature: RetroMines is a strictly non-custodial decentralized application (dApp). We do not store, collect, or have any form of access to users' private keys or seed phrases.

Asset Control: Users maintain 100% autonomy over their digital assets at all times. The developer cannot recover or access user funds under any circumstances.


4. GLOBAL ACCESSIBILITY & LANGUAGE POLICY
To ensure a seamless global user experience, RetroMines officially supports 8 languages, including Russian. To maintain legal and technical consistency, English is established as the primary (First) language for all legal documentation.

5. REGULATORY RESPONSIBILITY
Users are solely responsible for ensuring that their use of the application and their interaction with blockchain-based assets comply with all local regulations and international laws regarding digital asset management.
